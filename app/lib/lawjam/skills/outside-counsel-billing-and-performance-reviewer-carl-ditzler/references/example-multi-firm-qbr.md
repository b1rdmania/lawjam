# Example: Multi-Firm QBR

Use this reference when the user wants a quarter-end management view comparing multiple firms or panel providers.

## Example Prompt
Use $outside-counsel-billing-performance-reviewer to prepare a QBR comparing 3 outside counsel firms across litigation and commercial matters for our fiscal Q3 2026. Compare spend, budget predictability, OCG compliance, discount realization, staffing efficiency, narrative quality, and notable cost drivers. Use internal comparators first, keep external benchmarks directional, and produce an executive summary plus a one-page scorecard.

## Output Skeleton
### Disclaimer
- Draft only. Human review required.

### Quarter Summary
- total spend
- spend versus budget
- firms in scope
- matter mix

### Cross-Firm Scorecard
- compliance
- rate discipline
- staffing efficiency
- discount realization
- budget predictability
- narrative quality
- overall confidence

### Trend Highlights
- biggest quarter-over-quarter changes
- firms with improving or worsening patterns
- notable matter cost drivers

### Relationship and Panel Implications
- strengths by firm
- risks by firm
- watch items for next quarter

### Recommended Actions
- panel allocation questions
- pricing or discount follow-up
- staffing or OCG enforcement actions
- forecast or budget actions

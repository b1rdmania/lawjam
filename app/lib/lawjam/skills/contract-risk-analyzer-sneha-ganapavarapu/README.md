# Contract Risk Analyzer

A practical contract analysis skill built for founders and early-stage companies with no legal training.

## What This Skill Does

Analyses contracts across five critical clauses that make or break deals:
- **Limitation of Liability** — Financial exposure caps
- **Indemnities** — Who pays if things go wrong
- **IP Ownership** — Who owns what you create
- **Data Protection** — Legal data handling obligations
- **Termination** — Exit flexibility and post-contract obligations

For each clause, you get plain-English explanations, specific red flags to watch for, and negotiation language you can actually use.

## Who This Is For

Founders and early-stage entrepreneurs reviewing vendor agreements, service contracts, and partnership deals without a lawyer on retainer.

## Author

Built by Sneha Ganapavarapu, a lawyer-turned-product builder with background in IP licensing law, B2B SaaS product management, and legal tech. This skill draws from her practitioner experience and her contract risk analysis tool, Pactora.

## How to Use

1. Upload or paste your contract
2. Answer clarifying questions about the deal
3. Review the risk analysis across all five clauses
4. Use the suggested negotiation language to push back on unfavourable terms
5. Get a lawyer to review before signing

## Disclaimer

This skill provides analysis and guidance, **not legal advice**. Every contract is different. Use this analysis to inform your decisions, not replace professional legal counsel. You are responsible for what you agree to.
